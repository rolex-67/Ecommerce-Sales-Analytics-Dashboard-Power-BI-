# 📊 Ecommerce Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 🚀 Overview

Developed an interactive **Ecommerce Sales Analytics Dashboard** to monitor business performance across sales, profitability, product categories, and regional distribution. The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify trends, detect inefficiencies, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Track **Year-to-Date (YTD) performance** across key metrics
* Identify **high-performing and underperforming products**
* Analyze **regional sales distribution**
* Evaluate **profitability across categories**
* Understand **shipping preferences and their impact on sales**

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue & Profitability

* Total **YTD Sales reached $11.53M**, with a slight decline of **-0.83%**, indicating stagnation in revenue growth.
* **YTD Profit stands at $1.34M**, showing a positive growth of **+4.50%**, suggesting improved cost efficiency and margin optimization.
* Despite declining sales, increasing profit highlights **better operational efficiency or pricing strategy improvements**.

---

### 📦 Sales Volume Trends

* Total **YTD Quantity sold is 107.2K units**, down by **-7.29%**, indicating reduced order volume.
* Declining quantity with stable revenue suggests a **shift toward higher-value transactions**.

---

### 🛍️ Category Performance

* **Technology** category generates the highest sales (~$2.13M) but shows a **negative YoY trend (-1.37%)**, indicating potential saturation.
* **Office Supplies** experienced the **largest decline (-1.22%)**, signaling demand drop or competition impact.
* **Furniture** remains relatively stable with slight positive growth (+0.73%).

---

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

* The **West region contributes the highest share of sales**, followed by other regions with comparatively balanced distribution.
* Certain regions show **high sales but potentially lower profitability**, indicating scope for optimization.

---

### 🏆 Product-Level Insights

#### Top Performers:

* Products like **Staple Envelope and Staples** contribute significantly to revenue.
* High-performing products indicate **consistent demand and strong market fit**.

#### Underperformers:

* Bottom products (e.g., **Eldon Jumbo Box, Lexmark printers**) show significantly lower sales.
* These may require:

  * Pricing strategy revision
  * Marketing push
  * Inventory optimization

---

### 🚚 Shipping Analysis

* Majority of sales are driven by **Standard Class shipping (~60%+)**
* Faster shipping modes contribute less, indicating:

  * Cost-sensitive customer behavior
  * Opportunity to optimize premium delivery offerings

---

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

### 🔹 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling (Star Schema approach)

### 🔹 Key Features Implemented

* Dynamic KPI Cards with conditional formatting
* Interactive filters (Segment-based analysis)
* Drill-down capability across regions and categories
* Custom visual styling for enhanced readability
